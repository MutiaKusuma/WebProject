--About Me--

Long Short Story

Hi!!! I'm Mutia Maharani. Born and raised on the island of Sumatra, Indonesia. A country with a tropical atmosphere, I never knew what it was like to live with 4 seasons in a year. This made me curious and that curiosity brought me to where I am so far. I never imagined that I would slowly be able to achieve my dream of stepping outside of my home country. Now in Sakarya, Turkey, I am studying computer engineering at Sakarya University.

--Activities and Events--

I am currently studying in Turkey. More precisely at Sakarya University in the computer engineering department. Here I study using the Turkish language. Not an easy thing for me. However, thanks to the supportive educational environment and friends that always help making me enjoy my life here.

Collage Life

Besides being busy studying, I also actively participate in the Indonesian Student Association in Sakarya. We often organize events for the Indonesian students who live here. We help each other and become a new family in overseas land..

PPI Sakarya

Learning together and discussing the Turkish language is also part of my daily life. My friends and I often discuss and exchange ideas about Turkish in order not to forget it, because for us studying formally at TOMER for 10 months is not quite enough to master this language.

Learning together

The thing that I am truly grateful for since living here is meeting them, my closest friends who are like family to me. Without them I can't imagine how uncomfortable I would be living here. I often spend time and play with them. Telling stories about life and helping each other solve problems.
